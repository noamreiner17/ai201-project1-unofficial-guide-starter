Student question: Hi guys！ I am an incoming freshman and I really love playing piano in my free time. So I wonder whether there is a piano in the dorms!! Also, I wanna know whether we can choose our roommates and the dorms. Lastly, how’s the dorms? Is it necessary for me to rent the house outside? Thx!

Student comment: You can’t really choose roommates your freshman year. Shapiro Dorm had a keyboard during my tenure at Brandeis. However, Slosberg (music center) has a few practice rooms with pianos and is sound proof. Freshman year dorms are terrible but with a lucky number for the lottery, village/skyline are great dorms. Ziv and Ridgewood for junior year are great and Mods are fun for senior year


Student comment: The only dorms for freshman without an ADA exemption or some medical exemption are the Massell Quad and North quad dorms. There’s no AC in them and they are small for the two to three people they put in the room unless you get very lucky with a natural triple.


Student comment: You can’t choose your roommate(s) nor the dorm you live in (which I think is stupid imo but I digress). Honestly don’t lie on the roommate matching form please cuz I had a pretty shitty roommate experience. Dorms are pretty mediocre and if you’re in the basement, they flood every so often. Some mid-years get placed in village which is typically a sophomore/junior/senior dorm that has AC, but not many.
